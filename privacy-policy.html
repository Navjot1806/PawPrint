Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 1, 2026

PawPrinter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

In short: We only collect what's necessary to make PawPrinter work for you and your pet. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text)

Pet Information

  • Pet name, breed, age, and weight
  • Health records (vet visits, vaccinations, medications)
  • Activity logs (walks, meals, playtime)

Media

  • Photos you upload to the Memories feature

Device & Usage Data

  • Location data (only when you explicitly grant permission, used for activity tracking)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage analytics (crash reports, feature usage)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your pet's health records and activities
  • To provide reminders and notifications for pet care
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To respond to support requests

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Authentication through Firebase Auth with email verification

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

PawPrinter uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for secure login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore — for storing your pet's data
  • Firebase Storage — for storing uploaded photos
  • Google Maps — for location-based activity features

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Update your account and pet information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of notifications through app settings
  • Revoke location and camera permissions through your device settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (pet records, photos, activity logs) will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

PawPrinter is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of PawPrinter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
