Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Sign Up." Enter your email address and create a password. You can start adding your pets right away.

How do I add a new pet?

From the home screen, tap the "+" button. Enter your pet's name, breed, age, and other details. You can also upload a photo.

How do I track my pet's health records?

Select your pet's profile, then tap on the health section. You can log vet visits, vaccinations, medications, and more.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile > Settings and select "Delete Account." All your data, including pet records and photos, will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. PawPrinter uses Firebase with encrypted data transmission and secure cloud storage. We never sell your data to third parties.

The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. If it still doesn't work, contact us at the email above.
